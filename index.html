Why we exist

South Asian craft is everywhere. Credit for it isn’t.

You’ve seen the work — on runways, in lookbooks, in every “new” collection. What you rarely see is who made it first. Sathyaya is here to change that. We name the makers, tell their stories properly, and help buyers find the real thing.

What we do

Craft stories

Where the designs come from

Every pattern has a history. We trace it back to the looms, the dyes and the people, and tell that story properly.

Accountability

Credit where it’s due

When a brand copies South Asian work without credit, we report on it. Checked facts, named sources, on the record.

Designer spotlights

Films that reach real buyers

We make short films about new South Asian designers and put them in front of buyers in Australia and the diaspora — people who can actually buy the work.
